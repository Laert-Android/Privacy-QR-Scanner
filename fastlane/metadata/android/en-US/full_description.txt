Privacy QR Scanner is a free and open-source QR code and barcode scanner for Android that respects your privacy.

Features:
* Fully offline — no internet connection required
* No tracking, no analytics, no ads
* Scan QR codes and barcodes instantly
* Copy scanned result to clipboard
* Share scanned result with any app
* Open URLs directly in browser or compatible apps
* Vibration feedback on successful scan
* Clean Material Design UI with dark theme
* Supports all major formats: QR Code, EAN, UPC, Code 128, Code 39, and more

Permissions:
* CAMERA — required to scan QR codes
* VIBRATE — for haptic feedback on scan

No network permission is requested. No data leaves your device.
